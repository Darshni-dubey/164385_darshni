INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup sweet corn
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flour( Maida)
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • ½ tsp pepper powder
  • ½ tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for Frying
    • For tempering
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 green chillies/red chillies, chopped
  • ¼ cup of spring onions
  • ¼ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Salt to taste

METHOD

  1. Combine the corn, all purpose flour, cornflour, salt, pepper and 1 tbsp of water in a deep bowl and mix well.
  2. Heat the oil in a deep non-stick pan and deep-fry the corn till they turn golden brown in colour from all the sides. Drain on an absorbent paper and keep aside.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan, add the garlic and green chillies and sauté on a medium flame for a few seconds.
  4. Add the spring onion whites and greens and sauté on a medium flame for a few seconds.
  5. Add the tomato sauce, chilli sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, deep-fried corn and a little salt, mix well and toss on a medium flame for 1 minute.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve immediately.
